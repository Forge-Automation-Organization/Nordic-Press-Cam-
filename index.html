03 — The reporter

One desk, two crafts, three languages

A reporter and visual journalist based in Stockholm, Mohamad Assaad moves fluidly between the notebook and the lens. His weekly filing for Al-Kompis covers the politics, economy and social life of greater Stockholm — while his analysis on Greenland, Hormuz and the transatlantic order runs across the Arab press.

Nordic Press Cam pairs that reporting instinct with full production capability: cinematic event and institutional coverage, commercial shoots, drone work, and training for teams adopting modern, AI-assisted media tools. Multilingual by default, scalable by design — from a single dispatch to a full crew.
